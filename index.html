India

Welcome to India, a diverse and culturally rich country.

Festivals

Diwali

Diwali, the Festival of Lights is India's most celebrated festivals.

Holi

Holi, the Festival of Colors, is known for its vibrant celebrations.

Eid

Eid is a significant Muslim festival marked by prayers and feasting.

Places to Visit

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an iconic symbol of love and a UNESCO World Heritage sight.

Jaipur

Jaipur, often referred to as the Pink City, is known for its historic sites, including:

  • Amer Fort
  • Hawa Mahal
  • City Palace

Kerala

Kerala is famous for its serene backwaters and lush green landscapes.

Indian Cuisine

Butter Chicken

Butter chicken is a creamy and flavorful Indian dish.

Biryani

Biryani is a fragrant and aromatic rice dish with various regional variations. Notable ingredients include:

  1. Basmati Rice
  2. Chicken or Lamb (or vegetables for a vegetarian version)
  3. Spices (such as cumin, coriander, and garam masala)
  4. Yogurt and saffron for flavor and color

Dosa

Dosa is a South Indian crispy pancake made with fermented rice and lentil batter.
